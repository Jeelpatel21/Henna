Wood-burning art/Pyography

Wood-burning art, or pyrography, is the craft of using a heated metal tool to burn designs onto wood, leather, or other materials. It’s a versatile art form that can yield designs ranging from simple patterns to highly detailed portraits and landscapes. I apply intricate henna-inspired designs on wood in the form of wood-burning mandala art. It’s another medium of art that lets me be creative while I am not doing henna art.
